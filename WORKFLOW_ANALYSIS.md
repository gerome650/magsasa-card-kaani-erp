## KaAni AI Integration

### Core Capabilities:

#### **Field Assessment**
Analyzes farm conditions, crop suitability, and environmental factors to provide tailored agricultural advice.

#### **AgScore‚Ñ¢ Analysis**
Proprietary scoring system that evaluates farm viability and loan eligibility based on multiple data points.

#### **Risk Evaluation**
Assesses potential risks including weather patterns, market fluctuations, and crop diseases to inform decisions.

#### **Loan Recommendation**
Suggests appropriate loan products and amounts based on farm profile, AgScore, and repayment capacity.

### Example Interaction:
**User:** "What is the AgScore for a 2-hectare rice farm in Laguna with good irrigation?"

**KaAni Response:** "Based on the data for Laguna region, a 2-hectare rice farm with good irrigation typically receives an AgScore between 75-85 out of 100. This score reflects good production potential due to adequate water supply and suitable land size for efficient operations. Would you like me to provide a detailed breakdown of this score or recommend appropriate loan products?"

- Analyzes multiple factors: soil conditions, crop selection, weather patterns, farmer experience

#### **Crop Recommendations**
- Based on local soil conditions, climate data, and market trends

#### **Input Optimization**

- Based on crop type and soil analysis
- Helps farmers reduce costs while maximizing yields

#### **Yield Prediction**
- Uses historical data, weather forecasts, and current farming practices
- Provides accurate yield predictions
- Helps farmers and field officers make informed decisions about resource allocation
